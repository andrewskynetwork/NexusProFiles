Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Nexus Pro ("we," "our," or "the App") is a local scheduling tool designed to help users manage social media content. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

Nexus Pro is a local application. We do not host a central database. We only access your TikTok account data (such as username and video upload permissions) after you explicitly grant permission via the TikTok Login Kit.

2. Use of TikTok API

The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API to upload and schedule videos on your behalf. We only access the "video.upload" or "video.publish" scopes required to perform these actions.

3. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your machine. We do not transfer, sell, or share your personal data or tokens with any third parties.

4. User Rights

You may revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok security settings. To request data deletion of any locally cached information, simply uninstall the App or clear its local storage.
